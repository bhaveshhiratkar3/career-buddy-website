Design Philosophy

Career Buddy was created to bridge the gap between students seeking career guidance and accessible online resources. The goal is to provide **clear navigation**, a **professional visual identity**, and **mobile-first responsiveness** so students can benefit anytime, anywhere.

  • Color Scheme: Navy blue for trust, light tones for readability.
  • Layout: Minimal clicks to reach resources; clean card-based structure.
  • Typography: Sans-serif fonts for clarity and accessibility.
  • Responsiveness: Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
