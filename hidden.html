Design Philosophy

The Career Buddy website is designed to provide career guidance and resources in a clean, user-friendly interface. My main goals were:

  • Easy navigation with minimal clicks to access key resources.
  • Consistent color scheme using navy blue for trust and professionalism.
  • Responsive design to ensure usability on both mobile and desktop.
  • Clear call-to-action buttons guiding users to important sections.
  • Readable fonts and spacing to improve accessibility.
